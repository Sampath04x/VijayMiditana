About & Biography

About Dr. Vijay Miditana

Dr. Vijay Miditana is an Associate Professor and Head of the Department of Chemical Engineering at Jagannath Institute of Technology and Management (JITM), Centurion University of Technology and Management (CUTM), Paralakhemundi, Odisha — a role he has held continuously since July 2010.

He earned his PhD in Chemical Engineering from Andhra University, Visakhapatnam in 2019, after completing his M.Tech from SUVCE, Tirupati (SVU) in 2007 and B.Tech from GMRIT under JNTU Hyderabad in 2001.

His research spans mass transfer, electrochemical reactors, fluidized beds, nanofluids, bioenergy, and advanced materials. He has authored over 30 publications in international journals and conference proceedings, including papers indexed in Scopus and Materials Today: Proceedings.

Beyond research, he has guided two ongoing PhD scholars, served as Board of Studies (BOS) Chairman in Chemical & Petro-Chemical Engineering at Centurion University, and organized multiple AICTE-sponsored national-level programs.

Research Areas

Research Interests & Expertise

⚗️

Mass Transfer & Electrochemical Reactors

Wall-to-bulk and wall-to-bed mass transfer in bubble columns, fluidized bed electrochemical reactors, and two-phase flow systems with turbulence promoters.

🌊

Fluidized Bed Systems

Three-phase fluidized bed reactors, inverse fluidized beds, gas holdup studies, and coaxially placed structural elements for mass transfer enhancement.

🧪

Nanofluids & Hybrid Nanofluids

Synthesis, stability, thermal conductivity, and heat transfer augmentation of Al₂O₃–TiO₂ and other hybrid nanofluid dispersions.

🌱

Bioenergy & Biofuels

Biodiesel production from renewable biological sources, bio-oil from pyrolysis of biomass, ethanol fermentation, and characterization of biomass briquettes.

🏗️

Advanced Composites & Materials

Glass fiber-epoxy composites with CNT fillers, Al6061/Graphite composites with SiC reinforcement, and plasma sprayed thermal barrier coatings.

💡

Reaction Engineering & Kinetics

Kinetics of esterification reactions (propionic acid, butanoic acid with cyclohexanol), catalytic cracking, and process optimization studies.

🌍

Environmental Engineering

Fluoride removal from drinking water using nano-magnesium oxide, automobile wash water treatment using natural materials, and wastewater biosorption.

🖨️

3D Printing & Digital Manufacturing

Energy consumption prediction in 3D printing using deep learning models, characterization of additive manufacturing processes and sustainability reviews.
